Our Story

Mallory Consulting was founded on a simple belief: organizations deserve a project management partner who understands both the technology and the business behind it.

With years of hands-on experience implementing and configuring enterprise project management platforms, we bring a rare combination of technical depth and strategic thinking to every engagement.

We specialize in Planview AdaptiveWork — one of the most powerful project and work management platforms available — and we know it inside and out. From custom field configurations to complex workflow automations, we help organizations unlock the full potential of their investment.

◆

Our Approach

Every engagement follows our proven five-phase methodology: Requirements Analysis, Data Dictionary, Configuration Breakdown, Implementation Steps, and Test Plan.

Our Methodology

A structured, repeatable process that ensures quality outcomes every time.

01

Requirements Analysis

We start by deeply understanding your business processes, pain points, and goals. Nothing gets configured until the requirements are crystal clear.

02

Data Dictionary

Every custom field, object, and relationship is documented in a comprehensive data dictionary — the blueprint for your configuration.

03

Configuration Breakdown

We map every requirement to specific platform configurations, identifying dependencies and sequencing the work for maximum efficiency.

04

Implementation

Hands-on configuration of your platform: custom fields, workflow rules, custom actions, panels, and global customizations — built to spec.

05

Test Plan & Validation

Rigorous testing against documented requirements ensures everything works as expected before going live.
